1.What you need

To accompany any reading, we strongly advise you to settle down in a place that keeps you and in which you can read quietly. Take highlighters and post-its to annotate your books and remember your favourite moments and quotes when you finish the book. You also push to listen to some music, either with a playlist designed for the book, as there are many on music platforms, or with relaxing sounds.
